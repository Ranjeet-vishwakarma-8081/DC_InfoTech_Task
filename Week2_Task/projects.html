IQ-BOOST Mock Interview

IQ Boost Mock Interview Project aims to create a platform that mimics real tech interviews, focusing on coding challenges and behavioral questions. The goal is to prepare candidates for actual job interviews by simulating the entire process. The IQ Boost Mock Interview Project aims to provide users with a platform to practice mock interviews, track their progress, and receive feedback and also prepare candidates for actual job interviews by simulating the entire process.
